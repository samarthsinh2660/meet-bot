================================================================================
‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

1. REQUIRED FOR PAYMENTS
   These policy links are REQUIRED by Razorpay. Without them, payments will
   be blocked with error: "Payment blocked as website does not match 
   registered website(s)"

2. ALWAYS USE target="_blank"
   Policy pages should open in a new tab so users don't lose their place
   on your website.

3. SECURITY - rel="noopener noreferrer"
   Always add rel="noopener noreferrer" when using target="_blank" to
   prevent security vulnerabilities.

4. MOBILE RESPONSIVE
   Ensure policy links are easily clickable on mobile devices. Test on
   different screen sizes.

5. CHECKOUT PAGE IS CRITICAL
   The policy agreement on the checkout page is MOST important. Users must
   see and agree to policies before making payment.
